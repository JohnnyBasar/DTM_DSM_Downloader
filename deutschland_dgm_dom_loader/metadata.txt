about=QGIS plugin for downloading official German DGM/DOM raster data from federal-state open data services. 
    Current providers include Sachsen via GeoSN, Sachsen-Anhalt via WCS, and Brandenburg via direct OpenData tile access. 
    Additional federal states are planned for future releases. 
    The plugin supports optional tile merging, clipping to extent or polygon shape, loading results directly into QGIS, and optional CRS transformation/reprojection where required. 
    All datasets are provided by the official surveying and geodata authorities of the respective federal states and remain subject to their individual Open Data terms, usage rights, attribution requirements, and license conditions. 
    Users are responsible for reviewing and complying with the applicable legal and licensing information before use. 
    Please consult the official provider pages for dataset-specific license details and terms of use:
    Sachsen (GeoSN): Landesamt fuer Geobasisinformation Sachsen
    Sachsen-Anhalt: Landesamt fuer Vermessung und Geoinformation Sachsen-Anhalt
    Brandenburg: Landesvermessung und Geobasisinformation Brandenburg (LGB)

    The author assumes no liability for the correctness, completeness, legal compliance, or any consequences resulting from the use of downloaded data.

    For feedback regarding the plugin itself, please feel free to send an email.
